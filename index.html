Quick Review

The Vatic Prism Flash was my main paddle for a while, and for good reason. When the Prism Flash came out it was widely regarded as the best paddle for $100, and I still think that it competes in the price range today. This paddle is often recommended for beginners, and through my playtesting and experience, I can confidently say I would recommend this to most players looking for a paddle to upgrade from their Walmart paddle. While the paddle may not have the most power, the control is top in it's class. It is easy to control the ball and place shots where you want it. This paddle would be a great paddle for anyone on a budget or someone looking for a cheap, high quality control paddle.
